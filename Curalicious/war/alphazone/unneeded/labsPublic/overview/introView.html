The Idea of Space Labs

With Space Labs you can swiftly develop prototypes of web sites, mobile web apps, web designs, human interaction designs and much more. Everything is based on simple HTML, CSS and JavaScript and additionally you can develop some server-side APIs also using JavaScript (based on Rhino).

Share your prototypes easily with your friends, colleagues and customers and receive immediate feedback. The best is, you can do this without any installation or configuration, just request an invitation and let's go.
